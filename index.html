rebranding bodo;
CLIENT
Bodo is the company that designs and manufactures the world’s first microwave coffee roaster. In a world where coffee roasting is based mostly on feel and experience, we aim to provide an intuitive, scientific platform and bring coffee roasting into the new age with the help of quantitive data and experimentation.
OBJECTIVE
We are currently in the middle of a major rebranding in preparation for the Coffee Expo in July. We need a logo which will be used digitally, in-print, and on our products (possibly in the form of CNC'd badges). Ideally, this logo should be able to be incorporated as part of a design system in the future.
DESIGN
We are inspired by astronauts and the general concept of space exploration, and we see similarities between the two fields. Both require careful planning and scientific data, whilst at the same time pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. We feel excited when we see the NASA logo, and how its simplicity and fluidity captures the pinnacle of human exploration and technological advancement.

Seeing that microwave technology has existed since the 70s, a retro feel may also be possible.

In essence, we think our philosophy can be expressed by the following keywords: #innovative #intuitive #scientific #flexible #frontier #new #technological #retro
DELIVERABLES
We are looking for several (2-5) sketches/ideas for our logo. It doesn't have to be polished; it can also be hand-drawn (though preferrably vector graphics). Mainly we are looking for one of three following combinations:

a. Custom iconography + "BODO" typography (doesn't have to be all caps)
b. "B" iconography + "BODO" typography
c. "BODO" as logotype
COMMENTS
Here are some extra comments regarding the project. Throughout the period of a few months, we've been constantly thinking about our rebrand, with the crucial piece - the logo - always quite elusive... What we're hoping to achieve through this outsource is to find a spark that can kinda push us forward. We know what we want to represent as a company, but either through lack of time or skill, we fail to package that into a concise logo.

Regarding Bodo, what we're doing is quite interesing actually. Like we said, we're the company that builds the world's first microwave coffee roaster (as opposed to charcoal/natural gas/inductive heat sources). But more than that, I think we're increasingly disillustioned with the specialty coffee industry and hope to do our part in steering it into a scientific, fact-based, data-driven future.
COMPENSATION
